1. Logo: Animated with Framer Motion (pulse/gradient)
2. Icons: Hover animations on all icons (sidebar, buttons, cards)
3. Deep Shadows: Under cards, modals, elevated elements
4. Glassmorphism: Sidebar and top bar (frosted glass effect)
